Welcome To Canalla

At Canalla, we dream of creating a Peruvian-style taberna that captures the vibrant energy of Lima, the capital of Peru. Our concept features authentic Peruvian dishes served in a tapas-style format—known in Lima as ‘TODO PAL CENTRO’—paired with a highly curated beverage program showcasing selections from around the world.

In Peruvian slang, ‘Canalla’ is what you call your friend when they do something no one else dared to do. It’s bold, unapologetic—badass. Being Canalla means being brave.

We are Canallas

We aimt to bring a unique Peruvian experience, create a fun but elegant and inclusive community atmosphere and to bring people together over a modern take on a traditional Peruvian food.
